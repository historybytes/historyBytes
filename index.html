About the Show

Why History Matters

Every line of code has a story. Every algorithm has a creator. History Bytes digs into the human drama behind the technology we take for granted — from the first punch cards to the dawn of the internet.

Hosted by educator Karen Latimer, we explore the pivotal decisions, fierce rivalries, and unexpected breakthroughs that built the digital world.

Especially the ones history almost forgot.
